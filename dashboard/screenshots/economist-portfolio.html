Featured project

Philippine Employment in the AI Era

A labor-market trend analysis built from public data and framed for policy interpretation.

This flagship public-data case study examines employment, unemployment, labor force participation, and underemployment trends in the Philippines, then situates those patterns in the period of mainstream AI adoption. It is presented carefully as descriptive analysis rather than overstated causal proof, which is exactly the kind of methodological discipline serious institutions value.
